### Social Media Do’s and Don’ts



#### Do’s

\- Maintain a professional profile.

\- Share useful and informative content.

\- Use privacy settings properly.

\- Respect others online.

\- Verify information before sharing.



#### Don’ts

\- Do not share personal details publicly.

\- Avoid posting offensive content.

\- Do not spread fake news.

\- Avoid unnecessary arguments online.

\- Do not misuse others' content.
