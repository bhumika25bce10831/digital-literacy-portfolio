INTRODUCTION 
This project focuses on developing essential digital literacy skills required in today’s academic and professional environment. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the goal of this project is to create awareness about the effective and responsible use of digital tools. The project includes various tasks such as creating an infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration platforms, practicing professional communication, and understanding cybercrime. Through these tasks, I gained practical knowledge about digital presence, online safety, and communication etiquette. This project helped me understand the importance of using technology wisely and responsibly.
________________________________________
TASK 1: INFOGRAPHIC 
For this task, I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The infographic includes topics such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. I used simple language, icons, and visuals to make the content easy to understand and visually appealing. Canva provided various templates and design tools which made the creation process easier.
One challenge I faced was selecting the right content and organizing it in a clear and structured manner. I learned how to present information in a concise and attractive format. This task helped me improve my creativity and design skills while also understanding the importance of spreading awareness about digital literacy.
________________________________________
TASK 2: DIGITAL PORTFOLIO
In this task, I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. GitHub is a platform used to showcase projects and manage code repositories. LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where students can connect with professionals and explore career opportunities. Kaggle is useful for learning data science and participating in competitions.
These platforms are important for building a professional online presence. Over the next four years, I plan to actively use these platforms to upload projects, learn new skills, and connect with industry experts. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional digital identity and preparing for future career opportunities.
________________________________________
TASK 3: PLATFORMS 
For this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms. I used HackerRank to solve a beginner-level coding problem, which helped me understand basic programming logic and problem-solving techniques. The platform is user-friendly and suitable for beginners.
I also created a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” with five questions. The form included both multiple-choice and short-answer questions. I learned how to design a survey and collect responses effectively.
This task helped me understand the importance of online tools in academics. Coding platforms improve logical thinking, while collaboration tools like Google Forms help in communication and data collection.
________________________________________
TASK 4: EMAIL ETIQUETTE 
In this task, I learned how to write professional emails and maintain proper communication etiquette. I created two emails — one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for an internship. I followed a proper structure including subject line, greeting, body, and sign-off.
Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and create a negative impression. For example, sending an unclear or rude email to a professor can affect the relationship. This can be avoided by using polite language and clear structure. This task helped me improve my professional communication skills.
________________________________________
TASK 5: CYBERCRIME 
In this task, I studied phishing as a common cybercrime. I learned how attackers trick people into sharing sensitive information through fake emails and websites. I was surprised to see how easily people can fall into such traps.
This made me realize the importance of being careful while using the internet. I will now always verify links and avoid sharing personal or financial information online. This task helped me understand cyber safety and how to protect myself from online threats.
________________________________________
CONCLUSION 
This project helped me develop important digital skills required in today’s world. I learned about online presence, communication, collaboration tools, and cyber safety. Each task provided practical knowledge that will be useful in my academic and professional journey. Overall, this project improved my understanding of digital literacy and responsible use of technology.
